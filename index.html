Tanoor History

More than 20 years of culinary experience

A Yemeni restaurant inspired by the traditional clay oven known as the "tanoor," where generations have slow-cooked meats and baked fresh bread with deep, smoky flavor. At Tanoor, we bring the heart of Yemeni cuisine to your table—authentic dishes like mandi, zurbian, and malawah made with original spices and time-honored methods. Every bite is a taste of heritage, crafted with passion and served with warmth.
